Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Ancient Secured Vault is a protected knowledge and document reading platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Ancient Secured Vault app and website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the service:

  • Account information, such as name, email address, country, and sign-in details.
  • Reading activity, such as document access, bookmarks, notes, highlights, narration preferences, and reading progress.
  • Device and access information used to help protect documents and manage authorized access.
  • Payment or subscription status information when you request or use paid access.
  • Technical information, such as app performance, errors, browser or device type, and security-related activity.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Provide protected PDF reading, narration, notes, highlights, bookmarks, and reading progress features.
  • Control access to free and premium documents.
  • Improve security, prevent misuse, and protect confidential documents.
  • Process subscription or access requests.
  • Maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the app.

Third-Party Services

Ancient Secured Vault may use trusted third-party services, including Firebase services for authentication, storage, database, hosting, and cloud functions. Payment or checkout providers may be used when users request paid access. These services process information according to their own privacy and security policies.

Document Protection

The app includes security features intended to protect documents, including access controls, watermarks, reader activity controls, and screenshot prevention on supported mobile platforms. Web browsers may not allow full prevention of operating-system screenshots, but we use reasonable deterrents and protection measures.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, protect users and documents, or work with trusted service providers who support the platform.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, protect documents, meet legal requirements, resolve disputes, and maintain account records. Users may request account or data deletion by contacting us.

Children

Ancient Secured Vault is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that we collected personal information from a child without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your account information, subject to legal and security requirements.
